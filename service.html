Comprehensive Steel Furniture Solutions for Every Space

From design and fabrication to installation and after-sales support, Steel Furnimart delivers complete furniture services trusted by Sri Lankan homes, offices, and institutions since 1987.

Custom Fabrication

We design and manufacture tailor-made cupboards, lockers, racks, and workstations to fit your exact dimensions, color schemes, and functional requirements. CAD-based modeling ensures precision before production.

Residential Interiors

Transform bedrooms, kitchens, and study areas with stylish steel wardrobes, pantry cupboards, and modular shelves that combine durability with modern aesthetics. Our powder coatings resist moisture and corrosion.

Institutional Supply

We equip schools, universities, and hospitals with durable desks, beds, and storage units built to withstand daily wear. Bulk production ensures consistent quality across large-scale projects.

Corporate Furniture Solutions

From executive desks to filing cabinets, we design functional office systems that improve workspace ergonomics and brand image. Our teams handle layout planning, delivery, and assembly on site.

After-Sales Maintenance

Our service team offers on-call maintenance, repainting, and hardware replacement to extend the life of your furniture. We maintain detailed service logs for every client order.

Turnkey Project Consultation

Our engineers collaborate with architects and contractors from concept to installation—ensuring furniture integration meets safety, utility, and aesthetic requirements in both new and renovated facilities.

Why Choose Steel Furnimart?

With more than three decades of continuous innovation, Steel Furnimart has perfected the balance between craftsmanship and industrial efficiency. Every product undergoes stringent quality checks—from raw-material sourcing to final assembly. Our in-house design department works closely with clients to customise furniture layouts, material finishes, and accessories to suit both function and budget. Whether you’re fitting out a single home or an entire office complex, we ensure dependable delivery schedules, nationwide logistics, and responsive after-sales care.

Steel Furnimart

Since 1987, Steel Furnimart has designed and manufactured durable, elegant steel furniture for homes, offices, and institutions across Sri Lanka. Quality, reliability, and timely delivery are at the heart of everything we do.
