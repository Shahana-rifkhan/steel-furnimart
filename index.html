About Steel Furnimart

Sri Lanka’s Trusted Steel Furniture Manufacturer

Founded on 1 July 1987 in Colombo, Steel Furnimart has grown from a steel furniture trading business into a nationally recognised manufacturer. From our early showroom on Galle Road to our modern factory at No. 36/10, Paranawatta Road, Kerawalapitiya, Hendala–Wattala, we design and produce durable, elegant steel furniture for homes, offices, and institutions across Sri Lanka.

Quality You Can Trust

Every product is engineered with precision and strict quality checks to ensure long-lasting performance, strength, and a refined finish.

Trusted by Public & Private Sectors

For nearly four decades, we’ve supplied government institutions and leading private firms with reliable, customisable steel furniture solutions.
