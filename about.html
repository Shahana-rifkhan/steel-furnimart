About Steel Furnimart

Sri Lanka’s Trusted Steel Furniture Manufacturer Since 1987

Established on 1 July 1987 at Edward Lane, Colombo 03, Steel Furnimart began as a specialist in household and office steel furniture trading. By 1990, our move to No. 325, Galle Road, Colombo 04 accelerated growth and helped us become a recognized supplier to homes, offices, and institutions across the island. After eight years of hands-on market experience, we entered full-scale manufacturing in 1995 with our first facility in Dalugama, Kelaniya—bringing design, fabrication, and finishing under one roof.

Today, operations run from our own factory at No. 36/10, Paranawatta Road, Kerawalapitiya, Hendala–Wattala. Here, we blend modern processes with skilled craftsmanship: precision cutting, jig-assisted welding for consistent geometry, and high-performance powder-coated finishes that resist corrosion and wear. Our product portfolio includes steel wardrobes, cupboards, filing cabinets, office tables, lockers, student desk & chair sets, beds, racking, and a wide range of custom solutions for residential and corporate interiors.

For nearly four decades, we’ve supplied both the public sector and leading private firms with furniture that is engineered for daily use in Sri Lankan conditions. Customers choose us for predictable lead times, nationwide delivery, and a service culture that supports everything from one-off home projects to large institutional fit-outs.

Quality & Durability

We specify heavy-gauge steel, robust fixtures, and premium powder coats. Each batch undergoes dimensional checks and finish inspections to ensure strength, smooth operation, and lasting colour uniformity.

Customization & Project Support

From room measurements to layout optimization, our team delivers tailor-made designs—lock types, shelving layouts, cable management, colourways—plus coordinated delivery and on-site installation for a seamless handover.

After-Sales Care

We stand behind our products with maintenance, touch-ups, and replacement parts. For institutional clients, we provide service schedules and documentation to keep inventories in top working order.

What We’re Known For
  • Consistent build quality with jig-assisted fabrication and tight tolerances.
  • Powder-coated finishes engineered for coastal humidity and daily wear.
  • Practical designs: secure locks, anti-tilt drawers, adjustable shelving, ergonomic heights.
  • On-time delivery, careful packaging, and professional installation.
  • Responsive after-sales service and availability of spares.

Our Features

Pioneering Quality and Craftsmanship in Steel Furniture

For nearly four decades, Steel Furnimart has built a reputation as Sri Lanka’s most trusted name in durable, design-driven steel furniture. Our commitment to precision, reliability, and innovation is at the heart of every product we create.

Precision & Quality Checks

Every component undergoes strict inspection—from laser-cut panels to powder-coated finishes. Our quality-control process ensures uniform thickness, accurate dimensions, and durable corrosion resistance across all products. Nothing leaves our factory without meeting ISO-aligned internal standards for strength, finish, and usability.

Advanced Manufacturing

Our factory in Kerawalapitiya is equipped with CNC cutting, bending, and jig-based welding systems that ensure tight tolerances and structural uniformity. Each piece of furniture is built for long-term performance — from wardrobes and cupboards to complex modular office systems. This precision reduces waste and guarantees consistency even in large-volume orders.

Sustainability & Efficiency

Steel Furnimart operates on sustainable manufacturing principles — minimizing offcut waste, recycling scrap materials, and using low-VOC powder coatings that reduce environmental impact. Our energy-efficient curing systems recycle heat to lower power consumption, contributing to greener production and cleaner air.

Design & Customization

Our in-house design team collaborates with architects, businesses, and homeowners to develop personalized solutions. Whether it’s a modern office workstation, a compact apartment wardrobe, or heavy-duty industrial racks, we design around your exact needs — matching dimensions, finishes, and functionality for lasting value.

Customer Trust & Reliability

With a proven track record across government institutions, private offices, and thousands of homes, our name stands for dependability. Timely delivery, transparent pricing, and friendly after-sales support have earned Steel Furnimart long-standing partnerships throughout Sri Lanka.

High-End Finishing

Each product is coated using electrostatic powder paint technology for a smooth, glossy, and chip-resistant finish. The finish not only enhances visual appeal but also protects against moisture, rust, and everyday wear — keeping your furniture looking new for years.

Islandwide Delivery & Installation

We handle every step—from production to final installation—with care. Our logistics team ensures secure transport and professional on-site setup anywhere in Sri Lanka, minimizing disruption and guaranteeing perfect placement of your furniture.

Maintenance & Aftercare

Our commitment doesn’t end at delivery. We provide repainting, repair, and part replacement services to ensure your furniture maintains both appearance and function. Each client receives guidance on proper care and handling for extended product life.

Steel Furnimart

Since 1987, Steel Furnimart has designed and manufactured durable, elegant steel furniture for homes, offices, and institutions across Sri Lanka. Quality, reliability, and timely delivery are at the heart of everything we do.
